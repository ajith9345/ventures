UAE Business Licensing Category

DUBAI (DED) LICENSES

Dubai mainland licenses refer to business licenses issued by the Department of Economic Development (DED) in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (UAE), allowing companies to operate within the mainland areas of Dubai. Unlike free zones, where companies have certain restrictions on their operations and are subject to specific regulations, mainland companies can conduct business throughout the UAE and globally without limitations.

The Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC) is a special economic zone in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (UAE), designed to provide a favorable environment for businesses operating in the financial services sector. DUBAI (DMCC) FZ LICENSES

DMCC, short for Dubai Multi Commodities Centre, is a free zone authority in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, specializing in commodities trade and related businesses. It offers various types of licenses to businesses operating within its jurisdiction, including trading, manufacturing, and service licenses.

DUBAI (JAFZA) FZ LICENSES

Jafza, short for Jebel Ali Free Zone Authority, is one of the largest and most well-known free zones in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Free zones like Jafza offer businesses various advantages such as 100% foreign ownership, tax exemptions, and streamlined processes for setting up businesses.

DUBAI MAYDAN FZ LICENSES
